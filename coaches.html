Meet Our Coaches


Sofia Martinez

Ballet Coach

Sofia has over 10 years of experience in classical ballet and has trained with some of the best dancers in the world. She is passionate about teaching children the art of ballet in a fun and engaging way.

Emily Johnson

Football Coach

Emily is a former professional football player with a strong focus on skill development and teamwork. She enjoys helping young athletes build confidence and improve their game on the field.

Yuki Tanaka

Karate Coach

Yuki is a black belt karate instructor with over 15 years of teaching experience. She emphasizes discipline, respect, and self-defense in her classes, helping students build strength and confidence.

James Smith

Swimming Coach

James is a certified swimming instructor specializing in teaching children of all ages. His goal is to make swimming fun, safe, and a valuable skill for every child.

Clara Wilson

Drawing Coach

Clara is a talented artist with a passion for helping children express themselves through drawing and painting. Her classes encourage creativity and artistic exploration.

Michael Brown

Basketball Coach

Michael has been coaching basketball for over 8 years. He focuses on developing fundamental skills, teamwork, and a love for the game among young players.
